    Newton polynomials

    MatrixPolynomials.NewtonPolynomial — Type.
    NewtonPolynomial(ζ, d)

    The unique interpolation polynomial of a function in its Newton form, i.e.

    \[f(z) \approx p(z) = \sum_{j=1}^m f[\zeta_1,...,\zeta_j] \prod_{i=1}^{j-1}(z - \zeta_i),\]

    where ζ are the interpolation points and d[j]=f[ζ₁,...,ζⱼ] is the $j$th divided difference of the interpolated function f.
